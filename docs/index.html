Welcome to the Bodhi Garden

Welcome to the Bodhi Garden, a non-denominational Buddhist charity based in the UK.

Bodhi Garden means, literally, ‘garden of awakening’. It is for anyone drawn to Buddhist teachings, meditation practice, and dharma enquiry. It operates on the traditional Buddhist principle of dana.

The Bodhi Garden was established in 2001 by the English Buddhist Monastery Trust, a UK-registered charity (no. 1088804), whose object is the support the study and practice of the Buddhist way of life, and whose ethos is one of non-sectarianism.

If you would like to join our email list, please contact us at info@bodhigarden.org.
