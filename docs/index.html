Welcome to the Bodhi Garden

A Buddhist Charity Established in 2001

Welcome to the Bodhi Garden, a non-denominational Buddhist charity based in Brighton and Hove. Since 2001, we have been offering Buddhist teachings, weekend retreats, workshops and opportunities for meditation practice in the Brighton and Hove area. Feel free to read more about us.
